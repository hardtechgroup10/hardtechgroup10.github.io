To raise awareness of humanitarian crises around the world, we will partner with various NGOs to create education toy sets, allowing kids to engage in creative play and learn about various natural disasters and humanitarian issues from an early age.
Each toy will be developed to promote educational play and awareness from an early age among children, but also among the parents. Moreover, certain part of profits from the toys will be donated to the projects that seek funding.

build up nepal project

Since the earthquake in 2015, 5000.000 homes need to be reconstructed in Nepal. Build up Nepal is rebuilding houses in over 100 communities using CSEB technology. They combine innovative construction technologies, local materials and entrepreneurship to rebuild safe, affordable houses.
